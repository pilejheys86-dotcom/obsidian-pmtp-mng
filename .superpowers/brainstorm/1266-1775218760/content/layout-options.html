Terms Page Layout Options

Pick the layout that feels right — inspired by Laravel Cloud's legal page

A — Left Sticky Sidebar

Section nav anchored to the left. Content fills the right. Active section highlights as you scroll. Mirrors the Laravel Cloud layout exactly. Most space-efficient for long documents.

B — Right Sticky Sidebar

Same sticky nav but on the right. Content reads naturally left-to-right first. Common in documentation sites. Good for users who want to read without the nav catching their eye.

C — Sticky Top Nav Strip

Horizontal nav strip that sticks below the navbar as you scroll. Keeps the single-column reading experience. Scrolls horizontally on mobile. Simpler to implement.

💡 My recommendation: Option A — it matches the Laravel Cloud reference, makes the 13 sections very accessible, and your existing design system handles the wider layout well.
