Pawning Terms
Define penalty and service fee rules applied to all pawn transactions
Late Payment Penalty
Applied as a percentage of the principal loan when a payment is overdue
% of principal
Example: ₱ 10,000 principal → ₱ 350.00 penalty per overdue period
Service Fee Charge
Deducted from the loan principal at disbursement. BSP-regulated: must not exceed PHP 5.00 or 1% of principal, whichever is lower.
% of principal
⚠ BSP Cap: Actual fee charged = min(₱ 5.00, 1% × principal). The system enforces this cap automatically at transaction time.
Example: ₱ 10,000 principal → fee = min(₱ 5.00, ₱ 100.00) = ₱ 5.00
